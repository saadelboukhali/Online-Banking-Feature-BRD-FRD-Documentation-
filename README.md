# Online Banking Feature – BRD & FRD Documentation

## Project Overview
This repository contains the Business Requirements Document (BRD) and Functional Requirements Document (FRD) for an Online Banking feature designed for a bank website. The objective of this project is to enable customers to manage their banking activities 24x7 through a secure digital platform, improving customer experience while reducing dependency on call center operations.

The project addresses limitations of traditional banking hours and high call center wait times by introducing comprehensive online banking capabilities.

---

## Business Problem
- Limited branch operating hours restrict customer access to banking services.
- High call center wait times negatively impact customer satisfaction.
- Call center operations generate significant operational costs.

---

## Proposed Solution
Implement an Online Banking feature on the bank’s customer-facing website that allows users to securely access and manage their accounts anytime, reducing call center volume and operational costs while enhancing customer experience.

---

## Documents Included
- **BRD (Business Requirements Document)**  
  Defines business objectives, scope, assumptions, and high-level requirements.

- **FRD (Functional Requirements Document)**  
  Details functional requirements, system behavior, user interactions, validations, and page-level specifications.

---

## Scope of Online Banking Feature
- User registration and authentication
- Profile and credential management
- Account summary and transaction history
- Statement viewing and downloading
- Funds transfer and bill payment
- Check ordering and management
- Security controls and validation rules
